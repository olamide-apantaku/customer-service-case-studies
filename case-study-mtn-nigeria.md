# Case Study: Improving SLA Compliance and First Contact 
Resolution at MTN Nigeria

**Industry:** Telecom
**Company:** Ison Xperiences (MTN Nigeria Account)
**Role:** Customer Service Representative
**Period:** 2019 - 2022

## Background
As a Customer Service Representative handling the MTN 
Nigeria account at Ison Xperiences, I was responsible 
for managing 200+ inbound customer interactions daily 
via phone. The role required handling high volumes of 
customer inquiries, complaints and service requests 
while maintaining strict SLA targets and delivering 
consistent service quality.

## Challenges Identified
- High volume of daily interactions making it difficult 
  to maintain consistent SLA compliance
- Low first contact resolution rate due to complex 
  customer issues being passed between agents
- Customers requiring multiple contacts to resolve 
  a single issue
- Inconsistent service quality across interactions

## Actions Taken

**1. Improving SLA Compliance**
- Developed personal ticket prioritisation system 
  to manage high volume interactions efficiently
- Created structured response frameworks for 
  common MTN customer issues
- Educated customers on MTN products, tariffs and 
  service updates to reduce repeat contacts
- Monitored own SLA performance daily and adjusted 
  approach where needed

**2. Improving First Contact Resolution**
- Invested in deep product knowledge to resolve 
  issues without escalation where possible
- Applied active listening techniques to fully 
  understand customer issues before responding
- Developed consultative communication approach 
  to identify root cause of issues quickly
- Built strong relationships with internal teams 
  to resolve complex issues faster
- Followed up on every unresolved interaction 
  within 24 hours

## Results

| Metric | Before | After | Improvement |
|---|---|---|---|
| First Contact Resolution | Baseline | 30% FCR rate | Significant improvement |
| Net Promoter Score | Baseline | Improved | 40% increase |
| Customer Satisfaction Score | Baseline | Improved | 35% boost |
| Daily Interactions Managed | Baseline | 200+ daily | Consistent delivery |

**Results achieved within 1 month**

## Team Impact
The structured approach I developed for handling 
high volume interactions and improving first contact 
resolution contributed to overall team performance. 
My consultative communication style and product 
knowledge were shared with colleagues, helping to 
improve consistency across the team.

Building strong client relationships also contributed 
to improved customer retention and repeat engagement 
across the MTN Nigeria account.

## Key Learnings
- Deep product knowledge is the foundation of 
  first contact resolution
- Active listening reduces handle time and 
  improves resolution quality
- Consistent follow up builds customer trust 
  and improves NPS and CSAT scores
- High volume environments require structured 
  personal systems to maintain SLA compliance
- Strong internal relationships enable faster 
  resolution of complex issues
