# Case Study: Onboarding New Agents

**Industry:** BPO
**Role:** Customer Support Lead
**Scenario:** Onboarding and training new 
customer support agents at Imolle CX

## Background
As Customer Support Lead at Imolle CX, I was 
responsible for onboarding and training newly 
hired customer support agents. The challenge 
was ensuring new agents could handle live 
customer interactions confidently and independently 
as quickly as possible without compromising 
service quality.

## Challenge
- New agents had little or no prior customer 
  support experience
- No formal onboarding documentation existed
- Service quality needed to be maintained 
  during the training period
- New agents needed to learn CRM systems, 
  escalation processes and communication 
  standards simultaneously
- Training had to happen alongside live 
  support operations

## Actions Taken

**1. Developing Onboarding Documentation**
- Created a structured onboarding guide 
  covering all key support processes
- Documented ticket lifecycle, escalation 
  matrix and SLA guidelines
- Developed communication templates for 
  new agents to reference during live interactions
- Built a knowledge base of common customer 
  issues and resolutions for quick reference

**2. Structured Training Programme**
- Designed a phased training approach:
  - Week 1 — Product knowledge and CRM training
  - Week 2 — Shadowing experienced agents
  - Week 3 — Supervised live interactions
  - Week 4 — Independent handling with support available
- Conducted daily check ins with new agents 
  to address questions and concerns
- Provided real time feedback during 
  supervised interactions

**3. CRM and Tools Training**
- Walked new agents through full CRM workflow 
  from ticket creation to closure
- Demonstrated how to use macros and templates 
  for consistent responses
- Trained agents on ticket categorisation 
  and priority assignment
- Showed agents how to document interactions 
  accurately for team visibility

**4. Ongoing Support and Feedback**
- Held weekly feedback sessions with new agents
- Reviewed ticket quality and provided 
  constructive guidance
- Celebrated early wins to build confidence
- Created open door policy for questions 
  and support at any time

## Result

| Metric | Outcome |
|---|---|
| Agents Onboarded | Up to 6 agents successfully onboarded |
| Time to Independence | Agents handling live interactions by week 3 |
| Service Quality | Maintained throughout training period |
| Agent Confidence | Significantly improved by end of programme |
| Documentation | Full onboarding guide created for future use |

**All agents successfully integrated within 4 weeks**

## Key Learnings
- Structured documentation is the foundation 
  of effective onboarding
- A phased approach builds confidence without 
  overwhelming new agents
- Daily check ins during the first two weeks 
  prevent small issues from becoming big problems
- Celebrating early wins accelerates agent 
  confidence and performance
- Good onboarding documentation benefits the 
  entire team long after training is complete

- Documentation and knowledge base creation

- Leadership and team development
